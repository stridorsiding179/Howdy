## 📝 Usage Guide

### First Time Setup
Open the Howdy application after installation. You will see a login screen for your Roblox account. Enter your credentials. The app stores these locally on your machine with encryption. We do not transmit your passwords to any external servers.

### Adding New Accounts
Press the "Add Account" button in the top menu. Enter your username and password. The app will verify your information and display the account in your primary list. You can add as many accounts as your system memory supports. 

### Running Concurrent Instances
Select one or more accounts from your list. Right-click the selection and choose "Launch." Each account will open in its own window. You can resize or move these windows to suit your screen.

### Creating Groups
Drag and drop your accounts into the sidebar folders. Rename these folders to anything you prefer. If you have many accounts, use the search bar at the top to find specific names or groups quickly.

### Using the Scheduler
Click the "Scheduler" tab on the left side of the window. Click "New Task." Choose the account and the command you want to perform. Set the time and date for the action. Ensure your computer remains awake during this time for the task to trigger successfully.
